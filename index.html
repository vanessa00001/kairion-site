Our story

Where opportunity becomes legacy

Kairion Group takes its name from Kairos, the ancient Greek word for the opportune moment: the point where preparation, courage, and timing meet and a door opens. Kairos is the moment. Kairion is what you build once you walk through it, the work that turns a single opening into something lasting.

We help companies run better. Whether you need the thinking, the build, the people, or all of it, Kairion meets you where you are and stays until the work holds.

The one test

Is the organization measurably better, and does the improvement hold after we step back?

Most firms do one thing. We built Kairion as a parent company that brings consulting, technology, and talent together, because the problems worth solving rarely sit in a single lane, and the fix rarely lasts when it is handed off in pieces.

Three pillars. Engage one, two, or all three.

Consulting

We start with the operating model. Using Lean Six Sigma, we find where work slows down, where cost hides, and what to change first, then hand you a clear, prioritized plan. Some clients take only this and run it themselves; most use it as the map for what comes next.

Technology

This is where the plan becomes real. We build the AI automation and system integration that strips out manual work, connects your ERP, CRM, and tools, and holds up at enterprise scale. Some come to us for the build alone; many pair it with the consulting that shaped it.

Talent

Great systems still need capable people. Through the Kairion academy we train professionals, many of them Filipino, to global standards and place them where the work is. The academy builds the bench our own delivery draws on, so the talent we place is talent we trust.

Founded in 2020 and built as a parent company so these three pillars work as one, Kairion serves organizations worldwide. That is where opportunity becomes legacy.

Case studies

What the work delivers

Eight representative engagements, each with the live workflow behind it. Figures are representative models based on typical volumes.

Problem
Orders came in through email, e-commerce, EDI, and a web portal, and operations re-keyed each one across CRM, ERP, inventory, and fulfillment, coordinating by email and updating status by hand. Depending on complexity, each order took 10 to 15 minutes of manual work, invited errors, and tied the team up in routine processing.
Action
Kairion AI Intelligence monitors incoming orders, reads PDF and email purchase orders, and uses AI and OCR to extract customer details, SKUs, quantities, shipping addresses, delivery dates, and payment terms, then validates and de-duplicates into a structured record. The Kairion Automation Engine creates or updates the customer in the CRM, creates the sales order in the ERP, checks and reserves inventory, triggers fulfillment, sends shipping notifications and confirmations, and routes exceptions like stock shortages or pricing gaps to the right team. It also refreshes real-time executive dashboards and sends an AI summary of risks and delayed orders to leadership by email or Microsoft Teams.
Result
For organizations processing several hundred orders a day, eliminates thousands of manual processing hours a year, improves order accuracy, and gives leadership real-time visibility, while operations focus on exceptions instead of routine entry.
Stack
Kairion AI Intelligence reads and understands the documents, and the Kairion Automation Engine runs the governed enterprise orchestration across CRM, ERP, fulfillment, and reporting.
Live view: Kairion AI Intelligence reads and structures each order as it arrives, the Kairion Automation Engine coordinates CRM, ERP, fulfillment, and reporting in real time.
Problem
Repetitive questions arrived across live chat, email, and social, consuming agent time and leaving gaps outside business hours.
Action
Built AI support agents that read each inquiry, identify intent, check the CRM and knowledge base, answer common questions, draft replies, create and route tickets, qualify leads, and schedule meetings, escalating to a human only when a case is genuinely complex.
Result
Deflects 40 to 80% of routine inquiries, enables 24/7 coverage, lowers support staffing cost, and frees agents for high-value cases.
Inbound channels are triaged by Kairion AI Intelligence, with human escalation reserved for the cases that need judgment.
Problem
Leads arrived across web forms, paid ads, email, and events faster than the team could handle. Capture was manual, qualification was inconsistent, and follow-up slipped, so good leads went cold in an inbox.
Action
Kairion AI Intelligence captures every lead from each channel, enriches it, and scores intent with AI so the strongest opportunities surface first. The Kairion Automation Engine routes each lead to the right owner in the CRM, triggers a timely, personalized follow-up, books meetings, keeps the pipeline and reporting current, and escalates hot leads the moment they appear.
Result
Every lead gets a fast, consistent response, qualified leads reach the right rep while they are still warm, and more of the funnel converts into real pipeline instead of leaking at the top.
Stack
Kairion AI Intelligence handles capture, enrichment, and scoring, and the Kairion Automation Engine runs CRM routing, follow-up, and reporting.
Every lead is captured, enriched, and scored by AI, then routed and followed up through the CRM.
Problem
Ecommerce operations spanned NetSuite, Shopify, SPS Commerce, RF-Smart, Celigo, and multiple 3PL and WMS partners, with fragile integrations and delivery knowledge concentrated in one person.
Action
Owned end-to-end integrations and the EDI flows (850, 855, 856, 810, 940, 945), rebuilt and tested Celigo integration flows, supported NetSuite configuration and data accuracy, and documented every process.
Result
Kept 10+ EDI transaction types flowing reliably across trading partners and made delivery repeatable rather than dependent on any single person.
Celigo sits at the center, keeping orders, inventory, fulfillment, returns, and EDI in sync across every connected system.
Problem
Finance staff opened every invoice, keyed the data, checked the purchase order, sent approval emails, updated the ERP, and tracked payment status in spreadsheets, roughly 10 to 20 minutes per invoice.
Action
Kairion AI Intelligence reads each invoice from email, a shared folder, or a vendor portal and uses AI to extract the vendor, invoice and PO numbers, dates, line items, tax, currency, and total, flagging missing data, duplicates, and unusual values. The Kairion Automation Engine then validates the supplier against the ERP, matches the invoice to the purchase order and goods receipt, routes exceptions for approval, creates the invoice record, updates payment status, notifies finance or the supplier, and refreshes the executive dashboard.
Result
At 3,000 invoices a month, cutting even 10 minutes of manual work per invoice frees around 500 labor hours a month, roughly $12,500 monthly or $150,000 a year at a $25 hourly cost, before further savings from fewer duplicate payments, fewer late fees, and faster approvals.

Kairion AI Intelligence extracts and checks each invoice, the Kairion Automation Engine runs the supplier check, PO match, approvals, ERP posting, and reporting.
Problem
Purchase requests moved through email and spreadsheets, approvals stalled in inboxes, and someone re-keyed each one into the ERP. Spend was hard to see until the invoice arrived, and off-contract buying slipped through unnoticed.
Action
Kairion AI Intelligence captures every request from a form, email, or portal and uses AI to categorize it, check the budget, and confirm a preferred supplier before it moves. The Kairion Automation Engine then routes the request through the right approval chain, creates the purchase order in the ERP, receives and three-way matches against goods receipts and invoices, posts the result, and notifies finance and the requester at every step.
Result
Requests move from submission to approved PO in a fraction of the time, spend stays visible and within policy from request to payment, and finance sees commitments before the invoice arrives instead of after.

Kairion AI Intelligence handles intake and categorization, and the Kairion Automation Engine runs the governed procure-to-pay orchestration across approvals, the ERP, and three-way matching.
Kairion AI Intelligence categorizes each request; the Kairion Automation Engine routes approvals, creates the PO, matches receipts, and posts to the ERP.

Questions, answered

What exactly does Kairion deliver? +
Working automation and integrations, not just advice. We diagnose the process, design the target state, and build it with the Kairion Intelligent Operations Platform, then integrate it across your ERP, CRM, and other systems and hand it over with measurement in place.
How is this different from a big consultancy? +
You get senior, hands-on delivery accountable for the outcome, faster timelines, and no army of juniors on your budget. We build and measure, rather than leaving you with a slide deck.
Which systems do you integrate? +
Across your whole stack. ERP (SAP, Oracle, Workday, NetSuite), CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), ecommerce and marketplaces (Shopify, BigCommerce, Amazon, eBay, SPS Commerce), cloud and service tools (AWS, Azure, GCP, ServiceNow, Zendesk), and BI (Power BI, Tableau), connected through the Kairion Integration Hub.
Is the AI safe to use on business data? +
Yes. We use evidence-based, permission-aware AI where every answer is traceable to your data, and the Kairion Automation Engine provides governed control throughout.
What size of company do you work with? +
Companies of every size, from small businesses and startups to mid-market and enterprise, across ecommerce, manufacturing, healthcare, finance, logistics, SaaS, retail, and professional services, worldwide.
How do engagements start? +
With a consultation. We look at where your operation slows down, map a path, and if it fits, scope a build. Book a call to begin.
How long does a typical engagement take? +
It depends on scope. A focused automation can go live in a few weeks, while a larger transformation runs across phases. We scope timelines upfront and deliver in increments, so you see value early rather than waiting for one big launch.

By scope and outcome, not padded hours. After a short discovery we propose a clear scope and price, so there are no surprises. Smaller automations and larger programs are sized accordingly.
Do you replace our systems, or work with them? +
