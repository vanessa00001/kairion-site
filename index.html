Our story

Where opportunity becomes legacy

Kairion is named after the Greek word Kairos, the right moment where preparation, courage, and opportunity meet. Kairos is the moment. Kairion is the journey that follows once you take the chance.

Kairion Group began from something our founder kept seeing across her career. The Philippines is full of talented, capable professionals who lack access to global opportunity. At the same time, businesses everywhere hit the same problems: manual work, disconnected systems, and decisions that move too slowly.

Kairion was built to sit between those two realities and connect them, helping organizations run better through consulting, technology, and delivery, while opening global doors for skilled professionals.

It was never meant to be just a tech consultancy or just a talent firm. It is where consulting, technology, talent, and innovation come together to create opportunities that change things for people, businesses, and communities.

FAQ

Questions, answered

What exactly does Kairion deliver? +
Working automation and integrations, not just advice. We diagnose the process, design the target state, and build it on n8n, Workato, and AI, then integrate it across your ERP, CRM, and other systems and hand it over with measurement in place.
How is this different from a big consultancy? +
You get senior, hands-on delivery accountable for the outcome, faster timelines, and no army of juniors on your budget. We build and measure, rather than leaving you with a slide deck.
Which systems do you integrate? +
Across your whole stack. ERP (SAP, Oracle, Workday, NetSuite), CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), ecommerce and marketplaces (Shopify, BigCommerce, Amazon, eBay, SPS Commerce), cloud and service tools (AWS, Azure, GCP, ServiceNow, Zendesk), and BI (Power BI, Tableau), connected through n8n, Workato, Celigo, EDI, and custom APIs.
Is the AI safe to use on business data? +
Yes. We use evidence-based, permission-aware AI where every answer is traceable to your data, and Workato provides governed orchestration with proper controls.
What size of company do you work with? +
Companies of every size, from small businesses and startups to mid-market and enterprise, across ecommerce, manufacturing, healthcare, finance, logistics, SaaS, retail, and professional services, worldwide.
How do engagements start? +
With a consultation. We look at where your operation slows down, map a path, and if it fits, scope a build. Book a call to begin.
How long does a typical engagement take? +
It depends on scope. A focused automation can go live in a few weeks, while a larger transformation runs across phases. We scope timelines upfront and deliver in increments, so you see value early rather than waiting for one big launch.
How do you price your work? +
By scope and outcome, not padded hours. After a short discovery we propose a clear scope and price, so there are no surprises. Smaller automations and larger programs are sized accordingly.
Do you replace our systems, or work with them? +
We work with what you already run. Kairion connects and automates across your current ERP, CRM, ecommerce, and tools rather than forcing a costly rip-and-replace.
Who owns the automations you build? +
You do. We build on your accounts and hand over full ownership, documentation, and enablement, so your team can run and extend everything after go-live.
Do you offer support after go-live? +
Yes. Every engagement includes handover and enablement, with optional ongoing support so your automations keep running and improving as your business changes.
